Connect to Vrbo (via form submission)

Lodgify Resources Team
  • August 11, 2025 10:02
  • Updated
The availability of this type of connection is currently limited to some accounts. You just need to follow the instructions provided, and you’ll be guided through the steps based on the version you have.

This integration allows you to connect your HomeAway / Vrbo / Abritel / FeWo-direkt rentals to your Lodgify rentals. If you're not using an API connection, consider exporting and importing your calendar via iCal.
💡 Note: Even though in the following explanations, only the names of HomeAway/Vrbo will be used, all details regarding the integration apply equally to Abritel and FeWo-direkt.

Once the connection has been completed, you will be able to keep your calendar up to date by importing reservations and synchronizing availability. Your prices will be exported from Lodgify to HomeAway/Vrbo and you will also be able to export all your Lodgify rentals to HomeAway/Vrbo. Reservations will be charged directly through Lodgify, using our credit card gateways.

🔎 If you are searching specific information, do not hesitate to do a Control F (Command F for Apple) to search your keyword and get your information easily

💡 Notes:

  • The Vrbo icon shows once the connection has been set up on Lodgify side. However, in order to complete the synchronization setup, Vrbo must take steps on their end. Learn more.
  • Find out here how to disconnect from Vrbo.

Requirements

Please note that in order to be able to connect with HomeAway/Vrbo, you will need to meet the following requirements: 

  • Each Vrbo/HomeAway account can only be connected with one Lodgify account. 
    And each Lodgify account can only be connected to one Vrbo account.
    💡️ Notes:
      • If you do not have a Vrbo account yet, it will be created for you.
      • If you do not yet have rentals inside your Vrbo account, the rentals can be pushed from Lodgify into Vrbo for you.

  • Ensure that you have not listed the same property across multiple Vrbo accounts, as this is not possible with Lodgify.

  • Vrbo has the following VAT requirements:

    • EU and UK: valid VAT number of Tax identifier

    • Australia + New Zealand: valid ABN number / NZBN number

    • Quebec region: valid GST / QST number
      💡️ Important Note: If you are a Private owner without a VAT number, Vrbo will not proceed with the integration project.

  • Your Vrbo listings need to be set up as 'pay per booking' or you need to have an active subscription with Vrbo for all the properties.
  • You need to have a Credit Card payment gateway or Paypal active in Lodgify as all reservations will be charged via Lodgify. You can use Stripe, Braintree, Authorize.net, Payyo or Paypal. Learn how to activate a credit card payment gateway or how to activate Paypal.
  • Existing reservations or closed periods cannot be imported from HomeAway/Vrbo into Lodgify, so they will need to be created manually in Lodgify before the connection to avoid double bookings.
  • You need to use the currency of the master brand of HomeAway/Vrbo (EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, NZD, JPY) and you need to have the same currency in Lodgify. To edit your currency in Lodgify you can do so in the rental's Prices section.
    💡 Note: To prevent currency inconsistencies in your rental, it's important that the currency of your prices aligns with the currency specified in your policy. This could, for instance, include the currency used for your security deposit.

Please note that there are also content requirements for your Lodgify listings in order to be able to connect them with HomeAway/Vrbo:

  • Your rental must have a name.
  • The rental's name must be between 20 and 80 characters long.
  • The description must be between 400 and 10,000 characters long.
  • Your rental must have an accurate address and latitude/longitude coordinates.
  • Your rental needs to have between 6 and 50 high-quality photos, with a minimum size of 1920 x 1080 pixels.
  • You must have at least 1 bedroom and 1 bathroom photo.

💡 Note: If you choose to add a rental agreement, you must enter text. Adding a rental agreement in the form of only a link will stop the synchronization.

Connect Lodgify to Vrbo

To set up a new connection, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your left-hand menu and select Channel Manager and choose Vrbo.
  2. Then click Connect.
  3. Select Fill the form, you’ll need to carefully read the steps described in the form and execute the actions required for your rentals.
    Here is the form in an other language: English | Spanish | Italian | French | German | Portuguese 

⚠️ Important: If you are redirected to a page to log in or sign up to Vrbo, please follow the instructions provided in this article instead.

If you want to add a new rental to an existing connection, follow the same process.

Once you have submitted it, we will be notified and we will be able to start the mapping for you. We will send you an email to confirm the mapping has been done from our side. This will include a form with which you can finalize the connection with the HomeAway/Vrbo team.

You will then be contacted by the HomeAway/Vrbo commercial and implementation team to finalize the connection.

Fees and taxes ✔️

Fees will by default be added to every applicable booking. Optional fees must be set up as Add-ons in Lodgify, and then manually added to bookings once the booking has been imported.
Pet fees ✔️

 If you want to set up a pet fee for Vrbo, you need to:

  1. Create a new fee: set the Fee type to Pet Fee and choose a flat price, either as a single charge per stay, with VAT excluded.
  2. Edit the fee restriction to apply this fee to Vrbo only.

Limitations:

  • Pet fee configured as a percentage are not supported.
  • The "Short Stay Restriction" in pet fee settings is not supported.
  • "Per Guest Per Stay" or "Per Guest Per Night" pet fee are not supported.

💡 Note: For pet fees that include VAT, no separate tax is sent. For pet fees that exclude VAT, Vrbo supports sending taxes separately.
Promotions ✔️ 

Promotions that do not use a promotion code are synced with HomeAway.

💡 Note: If multiple active promotions are applicable to a specific booking without a promotion code, only the highest-value promotion will be applied.

Example: You have a last-minute promotion (10% if booked 3 days from check-in) and a length of stay promotion (15% if the booking is of at least 7 nights), a discount of 15% will be applied to bookings that meet both requirements.
Check-in / check-out time + days ✔️ Check-in/check-out days and times are exported from Lodgify. If you have not set check-in /check-out times in Lodgify, we will send 11 am for check-out and 2 pm for check-in.

Convert existing Vrbo bookings

If you've received Vrbo reservations through another channel manager before the connection with Lodgify, you can create these bookings in your Lodgify account. Then, a Vrbo Onboarding agent will prompt you to identify them as Vrbo bookings in your Lodgify account. This allows the Vrbo Onboarding agent to update the respective booking in Lodgify with the credit card details of the guest so that you can charge them for their stay via Lodgify.

Converting bookings to Vrbo is done in your Lodgify account. To do so:

  1. Go to your left-hand menu and select Reservations.
  2. Click Create booking and select Create booking with quote.
  3. Create the booking filling in the information from the existing Vrbo booking. When you reach the last step of the policy, please ensure you deactivated the policy and quote workflow.
  4. Then Save the booking.
  5. Next, set the Quote status to Agreed.
  6. Finally, click the three dots at the top of the page, then select Convert to Vrbo.

Frequently Asked Questions

How frequently does Lodgify update my prices and availability information in HomeAway/Vrbo?

Prices and availabilities are synced every few minutes. For content, HomeAway/Vrbo requests an update from Lodgify once per day.

For how long will the Vrbo booking request be valid?

You have 24 hours to either accept or cancel the request. Otherwise, it will expire, Vrbo may charge a fee, and the booking will remain in Unconfirmed status on Vrbo's side.

Can I generate a quote for inquiries I receive? 
Inquiries that lead to a booking request or an instant booking will be upgraded to a reservation with a quote. You should then direct potential guests to book through your listing, and you can then create a promotion in Lodgify to offer a different price. 

How can I make changes to HomeAway/Vrbo reservations?

HomeAway/Vrbo reservations can only be edited, charged, and deleted in Lodgify. New quotes need to be created in Lodgify and not in HomeAway/Vrbo. When the guest requests to cancel, you will receive a notification from Vrbo and you will need to cancel the reservation in Lodgify.
Learn how to edit a booking.

Where can I find my Merchant Name?

For Payyo, you can check your Merchant Name in your Account Menu. Select My Account and go to your Billing Contact details. If you have a Company Name, this would also be your Merchant Name. If you do not have a Company Name, your Merchant Name is your first name and last name.

For Stripe, Braintree, or Authorize.Net, please contact their support if you are unsure of where to find the Merchant Name.

What happens to future booking payments if I cancel my Lodgify subscription?

Some booking payments may be scheduled to be made after the paid-through date has expired, when you lose access to Lodgify. In this case, you must charge the customer's credit card before this expiry date or manage this payment separately from Lodgify or Vrbo, for example by asking them to pay the balance on arrival.
