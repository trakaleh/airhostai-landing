Contact our support team

You can contact us directly from your Lodgify account:

  1. Go to your Account Menu in the bottom left corner and select Quick Help.
  2. A widget will appear where you can ask your question or describe your request.
    Please be as clear and specific as possible, and include any helpful details to receive the most accurate and helpful response.
  3. If your issue isn’t resolved, you’ll be able to contact our team directly through the widget.

💡 Note: If you are in trial or are not an active user, clicking Quick help will connect you to our sales team.
