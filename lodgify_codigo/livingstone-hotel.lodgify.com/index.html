Discover the beauty of Palma

Come stay in our hotel on the beautiful coast of Palma de Mallorca.

In only a few minutes walk you can reach historical sights and the beach. Around the corner you will find several local shops and markets to enjoy all local delicacies.

Our hotel offers 3 different room types: single, double and junior suite to accommodate a broad variety of guests. All rooms are equipped with air-conditioning, wide-screen cable television, a set of soft towels and a fully stocked mini bar.

Guests can go for a swim at the beach, which is in walking distance, or cool down in the hotel's pool.

Every morning we serve a rich breakfast buffet, with freshly baked bread, pastries, a variety of cheese and local fruits.
