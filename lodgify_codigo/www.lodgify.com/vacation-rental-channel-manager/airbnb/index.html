All About Lodgify’s Airbnb channel manager

Lodgify’s Airbnb channel manager is an integrated tool that allows you to connect all your listings on Airbnb with your vacation rental property management software. By using a channel manager that works with Airbnb, all your bookings, calendars, and rates will be automatically synchronized with other top online travel agents like Vrbo, Booking.com, and Expedia.

Our full two-way Airbnb API integration will prevent any double bookings and keep your reservations up to date at all times. Not only does this save a lot of time, but it also cancels out the chance for human error when managing your vacation rental property!

How to synchronize your Airbnb calendar

You can sync your Airbnb calendar in just one step directly from the channel manager. Our channel manager seamlessly integrates your Airbnb calendar with other top online travel agents onto one centralized dashboard. When you synchronize your Airbnb calendar, our API integration shares all your information (bookings, availabilities, rates, etc.) between Lodgify, Airbnb, and any external listings connected to our channel manager.

By using our Airbnb channel manager, whenever you receive a booking on an external listing, the dates will be automatically blocked off on your Airbnb calendar. Instant synchronization means that errors such as overbooking or double bookings are far less likely to occur.

Why do you need an Airbnb channel manager?

An Airbnb channel manager can help you save time and increase bookings when managing your vacation rental business.

By using a two-way Airbnb API integration, you’ll be able to manage all your reservations from one place and save up to 20 hours of work a week. You’ll also reduce any human errors that might jeopardize your business. Because Airbnb charges a commission on bookings, you can also use our Airbnb channel manager to mark up rates and maximize your profit.

Preferred+ Software Partners of Airbnb

Airbnb has chosen Lodgify as a Preferred+ Software Partner (its highest partner status), signalling that our functionality and software connections meet or exceed their technical benchmarks. Airbnb’s recognition not only enables us to integrate our channel manager with the Airbnb API—it also comes with additional benefits we get to pass along to our customers.

Lodgify is committed to continually collaborating with Airbnb to improve our offerings for hosts and allow them to connect with a growing guest community.

What does Lodgify synchronize with Airbnb?

Bookings

Sync your reservations and guest details. We import the total booking amount.

Availability

18 months of availability will be synchronized and the calendar will be updated instantly.

Rates

Both default and seasonal rates are synchronized in Lodgify.

FAQ

What is a channel manager for Airbnb?
A channel manager for Airbnb is a software solution that syncs your Airbnb listings with your other third-party booking channels and your direct booking website. This enables you to sync availability, pricing, and other listing details across all your booking channels, preventing double bookings and helping you to better manage your vacation rentals.
Do I need a channel manager for Airbnb?
If you list your vacation rental on more than just Airbnb, an Airbnb channel manager is crucial to your business's success. Using an Airbnb channel management solution helps ensure your availability is always up to date and accurate across all your booking channels, preventing double and over bookings. The same goes for pricing and other listing details: You won't need to worry about making manual adjustments from different accounts, because your Airbnb channel manager keeps everything synced.

This not only saves you time, but also prevents human error and inspires trust in your potential guests.
What is the best channel manager for Airbnb?
The best channel manager for Airbnb is one that supports integrations with numerous other booking channels, syncs your availability as well as other listing details, and features additional automation and optimization features. You'll also want to look for a company that provides flexible pricing options as well as free onboarding and ongoing support.

How to sync Airbnb calendar with Booking.com?
If you want to sync your Airbnb calendar with Booking.com, you’ll need an Airbnb channel manager like Lodgify. This tool will allow you to automatically synchronize your bookings, listings, and rates via API integration.

To synchronize your Airbnb and Booking.com calendars, all you need to do is fill out an online form provided by our connectivity team. Once you have accepted the connection requirements, your Booking.com account will be connected to Lodgify. You can then sync it with your Airbnb calendar to manage all your listings from a centralized platform.

Alternatively, you can also log in to the Booking Extranet, and click on "Calendar." Select "Sync calendars" and then click on "Add calendar connection." Scroll down and click "Skip this step." Title your connection and click "Export Calendar." The last step is to copy the link and paste it into Airbnb’s "Calendar Address" field.

How to sync Airbnb calendar with Tripadvisor?
If you want to sync your Airbnb calendar with Tripadvisor, first you’ll need an Airbnb channel manager like Lodgify. This tool will allow you to automatically synchronize all your bookings, listings, and rates via iCal connection.

Alternatively, to synchronize your Airbnb calendar with Tripadvisor, you can also log in to Tripadvisor, and go to the "Calendar" tab. After selecting "Export Calendar," you will see your iCalendar link. Copy and paste this link into Airbnb’s "Calendar Address" field.
How to sync Airbnb with Google Calendar?
To sync Airbnb with your Google Calendar, go to your Airbnb dashboard and click on "Listings." Click on "Availability" and then under "Sync Calendar," select "Import Calendar." Copy the URL from Google Calendar into the "Calendar Address" field, name the calendar, and then click "Import Calendar."

Lodgify offers an Airbnb channel manager that will help simplify this process and allow you to automatically sync calendars from different channels into one centralized platform.
How can I connect my Airbnb listing with Lodgify’s channel manager?
To set up the connection between Lodgify and Airbnb, follow the steps below:

1.Go to your left-hand menu and select Channel Manager. Then click Connect below the Airbnb icon to sign in to your Airbnb account or create one.

2. Establish the connection by agreeing to the terms and conditions of the two platforms.

3. To set up a new connection or add a new rental to your existing connection, you will need to fill in a form providing some additional information to establish the mapping. Select the form in your preferred language (English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, or Portuguese) and complete it with the required information regarding your rental(s). Once submitted, we will start the mapping.

Note: You can also access the form from the Channel Manager section of your Lodgify account when you click on Connect to create a new connection.

4. You will receive a confirmation email once everything is set up.

Can I connect Lodgify to multiple Airbnb accounts?
Yes, Lodgify can connect to multiple Airbnb accounts. If you want to connect additional Airbnb accounts, go to your left-hand menu, select Channel Manager, and click Airbnb. Then, select Manage account and click on Connect another account.

Then follow the above procedure for each of your Airbnb accounts to connect.
