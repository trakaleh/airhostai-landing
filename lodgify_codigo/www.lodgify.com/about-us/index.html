What is Lodgify?

Lodgify is the comprehensive vacation rental platform designed for independent hosts and property managers, simplifying the management and marketing of lodging businesses.

It offers the essential tools to start, manage, and grow a rental enterprise, enabling users to create a bookable website, synchronize property data and availability across various third-party platforms, and efficiently manage all guest reservations and communications from a single, user-friendly interface.

Why work at Lodgify?

Lodgify is a scaling travel tech startup building Software-as-a-Service tools for the hospitality industry. It has received funding from notable travel tech and business angels.

Lodgify’s head office is based in sunny Barcelona, Spain, but we have employees from all around the world with hubs in USA, LATAM and Asia.

Our mission

Our mission is to empower the long tail vacation rental host or property manager worldwide to get started and grow their business through technology. Our ultimate goal is to make them independent.
