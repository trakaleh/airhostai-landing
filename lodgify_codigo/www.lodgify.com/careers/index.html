Why work at Lodgify?

Life at Lodgify

We work hard to create technology that will empower vacation rental property owners and managers to grow their businesses. Every member of our talented team has an important role to play in the success of our mission.

Our benefits

We're all about work hard, play hard! Enjoy the perks that will help you maintain that perfect work-life balance.

We empower

Not only do we empower our clients with the technology they need to thrive; we encourage each team member to take ownership of their work to be successful.

Modern workspace

There's never a dull moment in our bright, spacious, and renovated office in central Barcelona, near Sants' train station.

Shared values

We all believe in the same values, which is what makes our company culture so unique.

Regular fun activities

Enjoy daily football and ping pong games, weekly beach volleyball sessions plus regular off-site team activities in Barcelona.

Healthy snacks

Our kitchen is restocked weekly with fresh fruit, nuts, avocado toasts, and refreshments to keep you going. You'll also enjoy monthly meal vouchers to spend anywhere you like; especially in our Ametller Fridge, conveniently located in the office with a huge discount.

Generous vacation days

Your free time matters to us! And with 25 vacation days, you can visit family around the globe, or simply spend time relaxing on the beach.

Your health – covered

Keeping your health in good shape is a priority for us, that's why we offer private insurance for every member of our team.

Networking opportunities

Our highly skilled developers organize and actively participate in key industry meetups, events, and conferences.

Expert training

We care about your professional development, and we're committed to providing you with all the resources you need to continue learning. You can also step into the spotlight and share your expertise through our Engineering Blog.

Truly global team

Our growing team is made up of more than 320 people from 50 different nationalities.

Easy relocation

Whether it's NIEs or visas, we'll support you 100% if you decide to move to Barcelona. Plus, if you introduce a friend, you'll benefit from our employee referral perks.
