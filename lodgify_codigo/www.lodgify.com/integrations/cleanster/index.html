Cleanster Integration

Manage your cleaning service providers, turnover schedule, tasks, and communications in one place with Cleanster. A leading cleaning platform and app, Cleanster is the most innovative way to get your vacation rentals cleaned and guest-ready.

 

This integration requires both a Cleanster account and a Lodgify subscription.

Marketplace of cleaners

Imagine never having to post a listing or search for cleaning service providers online again.

Cleanster makes this possible with its marketplace of cleaners. Simply provide your property details to get started and start receiving bids from over 35,000 service providers. What’s more, you can rest assured knowing that Cleanster providers are vetted, experienced, rated, and reviewed.

Cleaning scheduling and management

Once you’ve found cleaners for your business, you can sync your Lodgify account and booking calendars with Cleanster to automate cleaning for the next 60 days. You can even protect against scheduling conflicts by setting both primary and backup cleaners.

Turnover management only gets easier from there: The Cleanster app makes sending and receiving updates and messages, reporting problems, and tracking inventory a breeze.

Photo checklists

Ensure properties are always cleaned to your expectations with Cleanster’s customizable photo checklists. Upload photos and instructions that communicate exactly how you want your property to be left so there’s no confusion.

Translation

Have cleaners on your team who speak a different language than you? Cleanster helps make sure nothing gets lost in translation by automatically translating your cleaning checklist, in-app messages, and important notes.

Remote access for cleaners

You’re managing turnover from afar, so why should you have to be there to let your cleaners in? Simply add your remote lock information to your property details to ensure cleaners always have the access they need.

Before and after photos

Check how guests leave your property and whether jobs meet your cleaning standards with Cleanster’s before and after photos. Simply ask your providers to upload before and after photos for each job they’re assigned to so you always remain in the loop.

 

This integration requires both a Cleanster account and a Lodgify subscription.
