  6. 3 Jun, 2025

    Reservation reports: Export key financial data

    New Feature
    Improvement
    Announcement

    You can now export a CSV file with detailed financial data for your reservations. This helps simplify accounting, tax reporting, and owner statements.

    To access the report:

    Go to Reports in the main menu, then click Reservation reports under Financial overview.

    To generate the report:

    1. Click Generate CSV.
    2. Select a check-out period (required).
    3. Apply filters like rental, booking status, or booking source (optional).
    4. Click Export CSV, and the download will start automatically.

    What’s included:

    • Room rates, fees, taxes, add-ons, promotions.
    • Cancellation fees.
    • Gross and net totals, VAT, and other financial details.

    This report includes all confirmed bookings with a checkout date in the selected range, and canceled bookings if the cancellation date falls within that range.

    Note: Data is available from January 1, 2024 onward. For reservations from external booking portals canceled before March 8, 2025, cancellation fee data may be incomplete due to limitations with historical data.

  8. 13 May, 2025

    Guest invoices for cancelled reservations

    Improvement

    We’ve just improved how invoices are handled for cancellations.

     

    Previously, invoices were automatically issued for the full reservation amount—even if a reservation was cancelled. Now, invoicing is more accurate and aligned with your policies.

     

    From now on, invoices for cancelled reservations will reflect only the applicable cancellation fee, not the full amount. This ensures your records stay clean and your guests are billed appropriately.

     

     

     

     

    Need to update an existing invoice?

    If an invoice was already generated using the full reservation amount, it won’t update automatically. You can easily fix this by heading to the invoicing section in reservation detail page and selecting “Re-generate” from the actions menu. The updated invoice will reflect the correct cancellation fee.

     

     

     

    Less confusion, better accuracy.

  10. 15 Apr, 2025

    Manage your automatic response languages

    Improvement
    Announcement

    You can now have more control over the languages used in your automatic responses. This update helps you communicate more clearly with your guests.

    What is this about?

    You can now choose which languages are enabled for your automatic responses. This ensures that guests receive messages in the languages you prefer.

    How to use it:

    • Select only the languages you want to use.
    • Set a default language for guest messages.
    • Easily switch between languages when editing templates.

    To customize your settings, go to Settings > Automatic responses > Manage languages.
